At the end of the study (i.e. in approximately xx weeks) we will determine the final earnings for each participant through a random draw. This random draw will be done in three steps

- Which set of allocation choices will be used (i.e. those with respect to partner 1 or partner 2).

- Who in this interaction will determine the final outcome (i.e. either you or your partner in that interaction)

- Which question (out of the 5) will determine the final earnings.

For example: to determine your final earnings, we will first roll a dice to determine which set of allocation choices will be used. If the dice shows either an 1, 2 or 3: it will be the choices with respect to your first interaction partner, If the dice shows an 4, 5 or 6: it will be the choices with respect to your second interaction partner. Once we have determined your interaction partner we will flip a coin to determine whether we will select the choices by you or the choices by your partner to determine the earnings. Finally, we will select one out of five balls from an urn to determine which of the five questions will determine your final outcome.

To insure your anonymity, neither you nor your partner will be informed about the choices by others nor about outcome of the random draw. You will simply receive a message concerning the total earnings you will receive from this study.
